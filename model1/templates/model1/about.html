About



This is the final project for the Interactive Data Science course at CMU, Spring 2017.
The team consists of Shao Ting Huang, Wendy Wei, Shaoqian Yang, and Vida Zhang.

The goal for this project is to help tourists decide where and when to go to have fun in Las Vegas by answering following questions:
Which neighborhood has higher/lower rating bars?
Which neighborhood has relatively high/low customer volume?



Product


Our targeted customers are visitors in Las Vegas. According to Las Vegas visitor statistics, there are more than 40 million people who visit Las Vegas each year. Our team used Yelp data to offers an interactive map as well as a recommendation system to help users to decide where and when to have fun.
